BetterAtlanta

Facilitating civic hacking in Atlanta by connecting people and projects.

We are helping people create value for their communities by sharing open technology projects and allowing them to contribute. Users can also use this resource for networking, research or recruiting.

Our intent was to 'graduate' a lot of ideas from 'Hack for Humanity' into Better Atlanta to along the community to be an extension of the proejct.

Project Details

Summary of what we’ve built and how you can help

Problem:
After many hack-athons (events where people come together to work on software/IT projects), we saw a lack of continuity with the projects and decided to provide a platform for others to connect to them and their projects.

Solution:
We thought, what better way to encourage participation than to expose these projects to the community and allow them to join, contribute, research or network.
